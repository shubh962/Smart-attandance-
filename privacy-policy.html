Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At Smart Attendance, privacy is built into our core philosophy. We believe your workplace operational data should remain confidential, transparently documented, and securely handled. This policy establishes how we collect, process, and protect data points across our application infrastructure.

Information Architecture & Collection

To deliver real-time cloud synchronization and reliable workforce validation metrics, the application manages the following variables:

  • Identity Profiles: Managed email coordinates, access roles, and system-defined naming profiles.
  • Operational Telemetry: Logged timestamps, verified presence counts, leave applications, and generated payroll sheets.
  • Cryptographic Tokens: Secure anonymous operational tokens to persist device states safely.

Location Telemetry & Compliance

Smart Attendance relies on intentional location lookups to verify operational geo-fences during critical interactions.

Zero Background Tracking Policy: Spatial positioning parameters are evaluated only during active user tasks (such as scanning a verification terminal or instantiating a company profile). The ecosystem does not track coordinates when the app is minimized, closed, or idle.

Hardware & Camera Interfaces

Camera access is requested exclusively to capture matrix security elements (QR codes). Image arrays are processed directly inside memory scopes locally on-device and are never streamed, stored, or indexed on cloud infrastructure servers.

Service Subprocessors

We coordinate with verified cloud providers to guarantee application stability and low-latency workflows. All transmission channels use Transport Layer Security (TLS):

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages identity state machines and credential gates securely.
  • Cloud Firestore: Provides structural multi-tenant storage for organization nodes.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: Records anonymous telemetry to pinpoint operational bugs and display performance metrics.

Data Security & Retainment Control

Your records are protected through structural rules layers mapped directly within our backend code. Organization administrators can initiate structured account termination routines directly via their terminal interface.

Age Boundaries

Our management platforms are tailored strictly for enterprise business workflows and corporate workforce operations. We do not intentionally compile structural entries for individuals under 13 years of age.
