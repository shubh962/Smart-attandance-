Privacy Policy

Smart Attendance respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

  • User account information such as email address and profile details.
  • Attendance-related information.
  • Location data used for attendance verification and company setup.
  • Camera access used for QR code scanning functionality.
  • Anonymous authentication data for guest access features.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide attendance and payroll services.
  • To verify attendance locations.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.
  • To maintain security and prevent unauthorized access.

Location Permission

Location access is used only during attendance verification or company setup. Smart Attendance does not continuously track users in the background without user action.

Camera Permission

Camera access is used only for QR code scanning and related attendance features.

Third-Party Services

Smart Attendance may use trusted third-party services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and related data through the app settings or by contacting support.

Children’s Privacy

Smart Attendance is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
