Data Deletion Policy

Smart Attendance respects user privacy and provides users with the ability to request deletion of their account and associated data.

What Data Can Be Deleted

  • User account information
  • Attendance records associated with the account
  • Profile information
  • Authentication-related data

How to Request Data Deletion

Users may request account and data deletion by:

  • Using the delete account option available inside the application settings.
  • Contacting support via email.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are generally processed within 7 business days.

Data Retention

Certain information may be temporarily retained for legal, security, fraud prevention, or operational purposes where required.

Third-Party Services

Some data may also be processed or stored through trusted third-party services such as Firebase services provided by Google.

Contact Us

For account deletion or privacy-related requests:

Email: support@smartattendance.app
