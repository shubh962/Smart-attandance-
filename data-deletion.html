Account Deletion & Data Purge Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Smart Attendance respects your right to control your digital footprint. We provide a straightforward approach to removing your structural records from our system indexes once your operational tenure concludes.

Scope of Data Purging

Executing a data removal process permanently drops the following parameters from our active multi-tenant collections:

  • Identity Scopes: User profiles, naming metadata, and secure registration keys.
  • Telemetry Traces: Associated physical attendance transactions, geo-fence matching metrics, and active check-in/check-out history.
  • Financial Sheets: Historic individual payroll logs compiled under the employee ID mapping.

Methods to Request Purging

Data termination sequences can be triggered via two clean compliance pathways:

Option A: Native In-App Execution
Navigate to your Application Profile Panel -> Settings -> Account Operations -> Select "Delete Account". This triggers an automated cascading removal inside Firestore.

Option B: Manual Desk Escalation
If you can no longer log in or prefer a system review, submit an intentional tracking request from your registered email node directly to our support desk.

Processing Timelines

Automated operations inside the application complete instant replication across server blocks immediately. Email escalations require credential verification protocols and complete processing routines inside 7 business days.

Immutable Retention Limits

Please note that workspace history, administrative operational company models, and corporate regulatory sheets might be retained under the active account of the primary employer entity if required for formal labor law compliance or anti-fraud verification.
